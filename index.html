Monique

Monique was born and raised in Oregon, and has always loved the beauty and grace that the lush wilderness of the Pacific Northwest provides. She was drawn to photography by the allure of capturing the moments of peoples lives, as they share in her love of her home state, and their love of each other.

Monique holds a Bachelor’s in Psychology from Concordia University in Portland, Oregon and a Master’s in Counseling Psychology from Lewis and Clark College in Portland, Oregon.

She lives in the Portland Metro area with her beautiful black lab, Maggie.
