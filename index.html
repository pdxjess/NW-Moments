Hi, I'm Monique!




I'm a true Oregonian, and have always loved the beauty and grace that the lush wilderness of the Pacific Northwest provides. I am drawn to photography by the allure of capturing the moments of peoples lives, as they share in my love of the PNW, and their love of each other.

I have a Bachelor’s in Psychology from Concordia University and a Master’s in Counseling Psychology from Lewis and Clark College.

I'm currently located in Portland with my adorable black lab, Maggie.
